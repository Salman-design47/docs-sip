## Supported Chains

**Active:**
- NEAR (native via NEAR Intents)
- Ethereum (EVM + stealth addresses)
- Solana (SVM + ed25519)
- Zcash (shielded pool)

**Coming Soon:**
- Arbitrum, Base, Polygon (EVM L2s)
- Aptos, Sui (MoveVM)
- Bitcoin (via chain signatures)
- Mina (proof verification)
